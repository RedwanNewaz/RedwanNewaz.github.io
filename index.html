Dr. Redwan Newaz

Biography

Dr. Redwan Newaz has several years of experience in robotics and autonomous systems. He is an experienced Post-Doctoral Researcher with a demonstrated history in designing and developing several autonomous systems including unmanned aerial vehicles, autonomous cars, humanoid robots and under water vehicle. His expertise includes but not limited to the following areas: modeling, localization, motion planning, path planning and mapping. He extensively studies control theory, mathematical optimization, probability and statistical modeling, classical machine learning and deep learning. Recently, he is focusing on deep learning-based pedestrian detection, tracking and behavior analysis for self-driving cars. His technical skills involve coding in C/C++, Python, Lua, Matlab, LabView and so on. His research interests include autonomous systems, applied machine learning, motion planning under uncertainty, optimal control, policy synthesis, model checking, and related domains. He serves as reviewer for top-tier conferences and journals in the field of robotics and control system.
